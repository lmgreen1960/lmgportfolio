Hi, I'm Lynn

Here's my career journey:

  • I became a CPA in my 20's;
  • In my 30's, I was a business analyst;
  • At 40, I was lucky enough to have the best job in the world...stay-at-home-mom;
  • Nearing 50, I pivoted into teaching, where I learned something every day from my students;
  • Recently, I have become a student myself with the completion of UC Berkeley's UX/UI bootcamp;
  • Annnd...we'll stop counting decades as I enter what will likely be my last career in the field of Human Computer Interaction....time will tell.

    As a teacher, I empathized with my users (hundreds of students and parents) while testing and iterating my original interactive visual designs (math tasks and activities) repeatedly during the day...with constant feedback from a live audience. What a great decade-long introduction to User Experience and the Design Thinking Process.

    My backgrounds in business and teaching, coupled with my completion of the UC Berkeley UX/UI Bootcamp, have launched me into my next career as a UX/UI designer.

    ​ As a CPA and systems analyst I gained the business knowledge needed to be a team member able to listen, learn and implement change. 

    Listening to students talk about their math thinking during my days as a middle school math teacher taught me to embrace multiple ways of thinking and problem solving.
